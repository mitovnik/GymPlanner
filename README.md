Gym Planner is a simple and fast workout planner and training log that helps you stay consistent and see real progress. Create routines, track sets and weights, and repeat your best sessions without wasting time.

Built for lifters who want clarity and results, Gym Planner keeps everything organized in one place with a clean interface and quick logging.

What you can do with Gym Planner:

- Quick logging for exercises, sets, reps, and weight
- Unlimited workouts
- Unlimited workout programs and templates
- Add your own exercises and notes
- Rest timer between sets
- Auto-fill your last used weights to save time
- Basic cardio tracking: calories, distance, and duration 
- Clear overview of your workout history and progress
